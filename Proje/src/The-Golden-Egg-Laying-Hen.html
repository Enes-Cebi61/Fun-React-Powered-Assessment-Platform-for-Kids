The Golden Egg-Laying Hen

The Golden Egg-Laying Hen

Once upon a time, in a distant village, there lived a poor farmer and his loving wife. This couple had only one hen, but this hen possessed an extraordinary ability. Every morning, the hen would lay a golden egg. The golden egg improved the farmer's life and made things easier for him and his wife. Thanks to this miraculous hen, the farmer and his wife began to grow wealthy. However, over time, the farmer started to dream of obtaining more golden eggs. He began to think that getting only one egg from the hen each day was no longer sufficient. Greed took over his mind. One day, the farmer wanted to see the golden egg inside the hen. He decided to cut open the hen to take the golden egg. However, when he cut the hen open, only a regular egg came out. The golden egg was in the hen's heart, forming only through its miraculous ability. The farmer and his wife missed their hen laying golden eggs. But now, due to their greed, the hen had lost its ability to lay golden eggs. The farmer and his wife came to appreciate the value of the golden eggs better, but it was too late.
